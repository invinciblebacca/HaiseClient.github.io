The Haise Client

The Haise Client is a relatively new ghost client that has been quickly growing in popularity. Our team of skilled coders have combined our unique skills do create a client that is virtually undetectable by both server anticheats and human moderators.

Features

  • Knockback

    Able to toggle knockback for important pvp situations.

  • Auto Aim

    Auto Aim for bow, to easily shoot people.

  • Ping Spike

    The ability to create your own ping spike for situations where bad ping is advantageous
